ABOUT US

Welcome to Lean Monkey co., where we believe in the joy and companionship that monkeys can bring to your life! Our passion for these incredible creatures drives us to connect loving homes with our adorable primate friends. At Lean Monkey co., we are dedicated to providing ethically sourced monkeys that are well-cared for and socialized. Our team is committed to educating potential owners about the responsibilities of monkey care, ensuring that each pet goes to a suitable environment where they can thrive. We prioritize the health and happiness of our monkeys, partnering with experienced veterinarians and specialists to ensure they receive the best care. Whether you’re a first-time pet owner or a seasoned primate enthusiast, we’re here to guide you through the journey of welcoming a monkey into your home. Join us in celebrating the unique bond between humans and monkeys. Explore our selection and find your new furry friend today!
